Erxes

Built from scratch a script in Node JS with Headless Chrome that searches for specific keywords in Google, finds a particular website, and engages with that website in an entirely randomised way (by scrolling down pages, clicking on links etc). Deployed to run every 10 minutes on Heroku Scheduler. Google SEO ranking of these websites rose drastically as a result.
